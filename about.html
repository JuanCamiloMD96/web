ABOUT

Emre Carpentry is a family-run custom-woodwork business based in Aruba

Located in Aruba, Emre Carpentry is a family-owned and operated custom woodwork business with a proud heritage of superior craftsmanship that has been passed down through generations of the Estimez family. As first and second-generation carpenters, Ahmet & Emre Estimez are committed to providing the finest handcrafted woodwork to their customers.

Over the years, Emre Carpentry has served a diverse clientele across the island and the Caribbean, including luxury residences, hotels, condominiums, restaurants, stores, and residential customers.

Our company provides our clients with a wealth of experience in working with hardwoods, traditional plywood, and exotic wood veneers, ensuring a superior level of craftsmanship and exceptional finishes. Our team of experts is dedicated to matching any existing finish or exceeding our clients' expectations, ensuring that every project is completed to the highest standards.
