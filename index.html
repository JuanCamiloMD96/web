Emre Carpentry

Emre Carpentry is a family-run custom-woodwork business based in Aruba. For over two decades, a superior level of craftsmanship has been ingrained in the Estimez family, who now own and operate the company. The father and son team, Ahmet and Emre Estimez, are first and second-generation carpenters who are proud to offer some of the finest handcrafted woodwork. Emre Carpentry has provided its services to numerous luxury residences, hotels, condominiums, restaurants, stores,and other customers in Aruba and the Caribbean, specialising in handcrafted kitchens, bathrooms, closets, custom furniture, and more.

What We Do
